Lamborghini



Lamborghini is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini.
Known for its high-performance, exotic, and cutting-edge vehicles, Lamborghini has become synonymous with luxury and speed.
The brand's cars are characterized by their distinctive and aggressive designs, powerful engines, and advanced engineering.
Lamborghini models often push the boundaries of automotive technology and are coveted by enthusiasts and collectors around the world.
